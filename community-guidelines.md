# Community Guidelines

We want this to be a respectful and constructive space for music discussion.

---

## Be Respectful

- Criticize music, not people
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks

---

## Be Honest

- Share genuine opinions
- No spam or fake reviews

---

## Copyright

- Do not upload copyrighted material you do not own
- No full song lyrics unless permitted

---

## Moderation

- Reviews can be reported by users
- Violations may result in content removal or account suspension

---

## Keep It Safe

- No illegal content
- No explicit or harmful material

---

Thank you for helping keep the community welcoming and respectful.
