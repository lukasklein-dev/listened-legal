# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18.01.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("vinylist").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Username
- Password (stored securely using encryption)

Email verification is required to activate your account.

---

### 1.2 User Generated Content

We collect and store content you voluntarily provide, including:

- Music ratings
- Reviews
- Profile information such as username and optional profile picture.

This content may be publicly visible to other users.

---

### 1.3 Usage & Technical Data

We may collect limited technical data such as:

- Device type
- App version
- Crash logs

This data is used solely to improve app stability and performance.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Display ratings and reviews
- Moderate content
- Improve app performance and security
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

All data is securely stored using **Supabase** infrastructure.

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data.

Your data may be shared only with:

- Infrastructure providers (e.g. Supabase)
- Legal authorities if required by law

---

## 5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Correct your data
- Delete your account and associated data

Account deletion can be initiated **directly within the app**. Account deletion is subject to a 14-day grace period during which the user may cancel the deletion by logging in again.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as your account is active or as required by law.

Once deleted, your data will be permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

The App may display music metadata provided by third-party services.

We do not provide full music streaming.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Changes will be effective upon publication.
